Instructions
  • Simon will give you the first signal. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color.
  • Simon will duplicate the first signal and add one. Repeat these two signals by pressing the same color in order.
  • Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.
  • If you fail to repeat the sequence exactly or if you take more than 5 seconds to repeat a signal, you lose!
